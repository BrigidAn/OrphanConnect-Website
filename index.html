About OrphanConnect

OrphanConnect is a mobile application designed to bridge the gap between volunteers and orphanages. Our mission is to make it easy for people to find orphanages, contribute, volunteer, and track the impact of their efforts. The app allows users to support children in need, and make meaningful connections with local communities.

App Features

Volunteer Opportunities

Find and join local orphanages where you can volunteer your time and skills.

Easy Donations

Support children in need directly through the app with secure donation options.

Events & Campaigns

Participate in charity events and campaigns organized by orphanages and volunteers.

Impact Tracking

Monitor how your contributions and volunteer work are making a real difference.
